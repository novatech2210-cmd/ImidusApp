# Milestone 3 Master: Customer Online Ordering Website

**Budget:** $1,200  
**Timeline:** End of Week 5

## Objective

Deliver a responsive, production-ready ordering website fully synchronized with the POS. Integrate Authorize.net for secure online payments and ensure orders, inventory, and statuses are mirrored in real-time.

## 🌐 Web Features

- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile browsers.
- **SEO & Social**: Google-friendly pages with rich meta tags.
- **Customer Tools**: Print-friendly order confirmations and digital receipts.
